Project Name: Doctor Gravity

Doctor Gravity is a 2D-Platformer inspired by the ever so popular Super Mario by Nintendo and also Gravity Rush by Japan Studio. My team and I took inspiration from both of these games. The art was mainly inspired by Super Mario and the gravity mechanics were inspired by Gravity Rush. The game has 4 levels including the tutorial. These levels offer a different variety of difficulty respectively.
