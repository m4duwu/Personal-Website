Network Generator


Done with Java, MYSql and OOP Techniques

Project Name: Network Generator

The Network Generator is a framework developed by my team and I during our internship as research assistants at Singapore University of Technology and Design. The framework is used to filter datasets from MetricsGrimoire Database Collection and Eclipse Database. The filtered dataset is then printed out onto a CSV file which will be then used to plot graphs via the use of R or Excel. The data is then analyzed to learn and understand how developers work together in a complex software development ecosystem.


With the use of the data, my Professors managed to publish a research paper titled "Clustering, Separation, and Connection: A Tale of Three Characteristics". The paper is published at 2021 IEEE International Conference on Software Maintenance and Evolution (ICSME). The link to the paper can be found here!
