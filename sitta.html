Role: Logistics Manager

My role in SITTA as a logistics manager is certainly an unique experience. Eversince SITTA was founded, the society has been self-sufficient and actively serving the Muslim population in SIT. Being logistics manager in SITTA has taught me how to stretch my resources and distribute them well. As you may or may not know, SIT has 6 satellite campuses and SITTA oversees all the prayer spaces across the 6 campuses. With that said, I was fully aware that I cant oversee the logistics and cleanliness of all the prayer spaces as the sole logistics manager. Hence, i started an initiative to recruit subcommittee members to help oversee the prayer spaces and to regularly maintain them. With the help of our community we managed and are still managing to be self-sufficient. Giving back to the community with likeminded individuals never gets old and it keeps me moving forward. A friend in deen is definitely a friend indeed. All in all this was one of the more unique and fruitful experiences that ive had as a logistics committee member as things were'nt as straightforward as i thought itd be.
