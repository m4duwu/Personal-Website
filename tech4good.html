Project Name: Rampability

Rampability is a dynamic bowling ramp which is designed and programmed to enhance the experience of bowling for people with Cerebral palsy. Rampability comes with a variety of functions to enable users to control elements of the game prior to launching the ball. Users will be able to move the ramp horizontally, vertically and angularly. The Dual Pivoting Function allows users to shift the slide from left to right, as well as steering the angle to approximately 30 Degrees left and right. The Height Adjustment System will allow users to raise or lower height levels of the ramp to their preferences. After deciding on their preferred direction direction of aim, a Spring Loaded Function will enable the bowling ball to be pushed down the slide into the bowling lane. All these functions can be accessed by buttons or a remote.
